About Us

Our Mission

Avalon does not center its focus purely around the future of automobiles, but rather the future of transportation as a whole. With carbon emissions rising to an unparalleled level, now has become a call to action for the matter. The necessity for automobiles at no time will dwindle. Millions are purchased, millions are scrapped each day. This proves a budding issue as the more people that have access to cars, the higher the rate of carbon emissions. But what if it doesn’t have to be that way? Avalon offers up a solution to this problem with its products and designs. Although environmentally driven, each car still appears to be sleek and relevant enough to stay on the market instead of a passing fad. With all the abilities and aspects that a gasoline run car has, it even proves to benefit users in the long run, as fully charging one of the cars is less than 75% of what it costs to fill up a tank of gas on average.
