Awards


Explore Boyertown TSA's lineup of awards and achievements over the past few years at every level of competition

Inspiration Award - States

BASH-TSA won the Pennsylvania State Inspiration Award in both 2017 and 2019 for having the most positive community involvement of any chapter in the state of Pennsylvania.

Purple Award

The Technology Student Association's National Service Project partner is the American Cancer Society; one of TSA's primary goals is to raise money for ACS efforts and research. BASH-TSA won the Purple Award at the national conference the past three years in a row for raising over $2,000 each year for the American Cancer Society to forward cancer research.

Inspiration Award - Regionals

BASH-TSA won the Pennsylvania Region 9 Inspiration Award in 2017 and 2019 for its outstanding involvement in the school community, regional conference planning, and chapter fundraising.

Regional Conference

At the regional level, BASH-TSA semi-finalized in 70 events and took top 3's in 42 events. This represented the highest achievement level of any chapter present.

State Conference

At the state level, BASH-TSA semi-finalized in 35 events and took top 3's in 15 events.

Nationals Conference

At the 2018 National Conference, BASH-TSA were finalists in many events including 6th place in Chapter Team, 4th in Scientific Visualization, and 5th in Software Development.
