Technology

All-electric. For all environments.

At the heart of every Avalon vehicle is our specially designed flexible aluminium chassis. It holds our dual motor, independent air suspension, intelligent battery management system, and remarkarble cabin interior. This thin, compact design with its symmetrical layout and low center of gravity provides ride comfort like no other automobile. With remarkable power, superior handling. impressive wheel articulation, and extra storage space where an internal combustion engine and drivetrain would normally sit, every Avalon vehicle is built with the goal of perfection.

Dual Motor Set Up

The Avalon electric drive delivers instant power and torque exactly where it's needed through three independent motors, with more than 200 horsepower available at each wheel for even our lowest level trim.




Independent Air Suspension

Accustomed to any road conditions, each model by Avalon features adaptive air suspension dampers to give the smoothest ride possible to all passengers.




Battery

Each creation of Avalon features a lithium ion battery, containing a range of battery sizes. With this form of energy, the car is given an advantage in acceleration, surpassing all others on the road. A battery management system is also incorporated into the design in order to learn from how the owner drives and optimize how power is used in each car.




Interior Design

The interior of Avalon cars is customizable to suit the owner and is meant to provide the ultimate driving experience. They are sleek and uncluttered, providing a luxurious feeling while remaining minimal in design and preventing distractions from the road.
